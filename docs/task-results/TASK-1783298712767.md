## Summary

Apex Scheduler has been rebuilt as a self-serve, multi-tenant SaaS platform. Each business gets isolated data via `business_id` scoping and Supabase RLS. Owners onboard on first login, invite staff by code, configure roles, and manage locations. Freemium gates cap free tier at 10 staff and 1 location; Pro unlocks unlimited staff, multiple locations, and advanced reporting. All Jigsy/brewpub branding has been removed.
