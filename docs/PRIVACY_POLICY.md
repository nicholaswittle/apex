# Apex Scheduler Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 6, 2026  
**App:** Apex Scheduler (`com.wisense.apex`)  
**Operator:** WiSense / Jigsy's Brewpub scheduling team

## Overview

Apex Scheduler is a staff scheduling app for hospitality teams. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

## Information we collect

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Email address | Account sign-in and team identification |
| Name | Display on schedules, swaps, and time cards |
| Work schedule data | Shifts, availability, swaps, sidework, and time-off requests |
| Time clock entries | Clock-in/out timestamps for payroll export |
| Push notification token (optional) | Schedule and approval alerts |
| Payment information | Processed by Stripe for owner subscriptions only; we do not store card numbers |

## How we use data

- Provide scheduling, swap, sidework, and time-off features
- Send in-app and push notifications for schedule changes and approvals
- Calculate labor cost estimates and export time cards for owners
- Manage owner subscription billing through Stripe

## Third-party services

| Service | Role |
|---------|------|
| [Supabase](https://supabase.com) | Authentication, database, and backend functions |
| [Firebase Cloud Messaging](https://firebase.google.com/products/cloud-messaging) | Push notifications |
| [Stripe](https://stripe.com) | Owner subscription payments |

Each provider processes data under their own privacy terms.

## Data retention

Schedule and time-entry data is retained while your organization uses Apex Scheduler. You may request deletion of your account by contacting your organization owner or app support.

## Security

- Row Level Security (RLS) restricts data access by organization and role
- Passwords are handled by Supabase Auth
- Subscription status cannot be modified directly by client apps

## Children's privacy

Apex Scheduler is intended for workplace use by staff aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Your choices

- You can disable push notifications in device settings
- Staff can update availability and submit time-off requests in-app
- Owners manage team access via invite codes

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact your organization administrator or the Apex Scheduler support contact listed in your App Store / Play Store listing.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected in an updated effective date above.
